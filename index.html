About

Welcome to Yarned Over! Here at Spittin and Shinning Llama Farm we have so much appreciation for our customers.

So much appreciation that we are offering this education site to create projects with the yarn they buy from the farm.

The projects range from easy to hard and there is even a randomly generated project as a bonus. Enjoy!
